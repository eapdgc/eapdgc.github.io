  2. Supporting Consortiums

    1. The International Parkinson Disease Genomics Consortium (IPDGC)

      Majority of research studies, genetics included, are based on population of European ancestry that makes up a minority of the world’s population. The International Parkinson Disease Genomics Consortium (IPDGC) recognised an imbalance in scientific research and data. Hence, in 2018, IPDGC proposed targeted efforts should be made to study the ‘underrepresented populations’ (in terms of research data). IPDGC helped to shape the formation of EAPDGC and continues to support our research efforts.
