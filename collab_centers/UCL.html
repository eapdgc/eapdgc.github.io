  • Kin Ying MOK

    • Dr Mok is a neurologist in Hong Kong. Dr Mok’s main research interest is neurogenetics and currently focus in following main areas.

    The first area is Parkinson disease (PD) related genetic study. He is the Principal Investigator in the East Asian Parkinson Disease Genomics Consortium. The consortium is collecting PD cases and control for genetic studies. This is in collaboration with other under-represented populations for PD genetics study.

    The second is Alzheimer’s disease (AD). Dr Mok works in the genetics stream in LonDownS Consortium studying risk of AD among the Down Syndrome subjects. Dr Mok is a co-PI of a project in Hong Kong to setup a longitudinal clinical cohort of early AD, aiming to build database of Han-Chinese biomarkers and its correlation with AD. Dr Mok is part of the team that receivedan Innovation Grant from HKSAR government to establish a joint research center on neurodegenerative disease under Hong Kong University of Science and Technology and UCL. The Center shall work with multiple Asian collaborators in AD research.

    At present, Dr Mok is the Principal Research Fellow in UK Dementia Research Institute at UCL and UCL Queen Square ION Department of Neurodegenerative Disease. He is also the Adjunct Associate Professor at Division of Life Science, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology and researcher at Hong Kong Centre of Neurodegenerative Disease (Health@InnoHK project)
